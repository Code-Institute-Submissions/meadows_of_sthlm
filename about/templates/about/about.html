About Us

Meadows Of Sthlm

Welcome to Meadows of Sthlm, your number one source for all things Jewelry. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of rings, neclaces, earrings and bracelets, with a focus on durability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2020 by Lisa Anderesson, Meadows Of Sthlm has come a long way from its beginnings in a toolshed in outskirts of Stockholm. When Lisa first started out, her passion for others be more eco-friendly while still staying fashinable and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the US and Europe and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly wing of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Lisa, CEO
